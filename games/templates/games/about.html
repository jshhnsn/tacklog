Welcome to Tacklog!

Embark on a journey to conquer your ever-growing video game backlog with Tacklog, where our mission is to help you tackle each title, one game at a time. Our name, a blend of "tackle" and "backlog," represents our commitment to helping you navigate, organize, and enjoy your personal gaming odyssey.

Why Tacklog?

  • Track your progress: With Tacklog, list the games you own, the ones you're diving into, and the adventures you've taken. Keep a clear record of where you've been and plot the path to where you're headed next.
  • Organize with ease: Our intuitive interface makes it simple to categorize your games, mark your progress, and celebrate each completion.
  • Stay motivated: Watch your backlog shrink as you progress with stats and visuals that showcase your journey from overwhelmed to overjoyed.

Join us at Tacklog and turn that daunting mountain of games into a series of delightful escapades. It's time to take control, play smart, and enjoy the ride. Your adventure awaits.
